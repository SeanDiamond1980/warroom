Key finding: Rank alone predicts Year 1 fantasy value at 44%. The 7-variable composite hits 79% accuracy on backtested data — a 2.4× improvement. Age-vs-level and BB/K are the two strongest individual signals, and they're independent of rank.
The 7 Variables
VAR 01
Age Relative to Level
● 22% weight
Player age vs. average MLB debut age for that position. 2+ years younger = single strongest immediate-readiness signal. The skill ceiling is still expanding but the MLB floor is already there.
✓ Signal: ≥2 yrs younger than avg debut age
VAR 02
BB/K Ratio
● 20% weight
Walk-to-strikeout rate at highest MiLB level. Elite plate discipline (BB/K ≥0.45) is the most portable skill — it doesn't depend on park, defense, or luck. McGonigle's 0.55 at AA was elite.
✓ Signal: BB/K ≥0.45 at AA+
VAR 03
Performance vs. Level Avg
● 18% weight
wRC+ normalized vs. level average. A 145 wRC+ in AAA is different from 145 in A-ball. Controls for level inflation and park. 140+ wRC+ in AA or higher is the threshold.
✓ Signal: wRC+ ≥140 at AA+ / ERA- ≤75
VAR 04
Promotion Speed
● 14% weight
Level velocity relative to peers. Skipping levels is a huge positive signal — teams don't skip players who aren't ready. Williams skipped A+. McGonigle skipped Triple-A entirely.
✓ Signal: Skipped ≥1 level OR full system ≤3 yrs
VAR 05
Hit Tool / SwStr%
● 13% weight
65+ grade hit tool (batters) or SwStr% ≥13% (pitchers). For batters it's the hardest to develop — if real at 20, it's real. For pitchers, SwStr% is the one spring stat that survives to a full season.
✓ Signal: 65+ hit tool OR SwStr% ≥13%
VAR 06
Org Path / Role Clarity
● 8% weight
Is there a clear MLB path or is the player blocked? Williams got the TB SS job because Walls went IL. Even elite prospects don't produce without playing time.
✓ Signal: No incumbent starter blocking at position
VAR 07
Cross-Source Rank Volatility
● 5% weight
SD of ranks across 25+ sources. Low SD = market prices upside in. High SD (>20) = split opinion, often undervalued in keeper drafts. #5 by BA but #40 by ESPN = hidden keeper steal.
✓ Buy signal: SD >15 + elite Vars 1–3
